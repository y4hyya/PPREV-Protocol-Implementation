1 · Register transaction (φ_R)

Lister submits transaction parameters and a notary attestation. The contract computes Ctx=H(txData ‖ policyId_R ‖ salt) and uses it as the txID.

2 · Apply & Engage (φ_A)

A counterparty submits an application (status PENDING). The listing owner subsequently calls engage to lock the listing.

3 · Settle / Expire (φ_S)

Listing owner settles before expiresAt; otherwise anyone calls expire and the counterparty is refunded with a 10% slash of remaining collateral (compounding).
